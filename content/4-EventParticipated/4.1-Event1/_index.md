# AWS Study Group Workshop - AI in Development

## Event Information

**Event Name:** AWS Study Group Workshop - AI in Development Lifecycle

**Date & Time:** May 9, 2026

**Location:** 26th Floor, Bitexco Tower, 02 Hai Trieu Street, Saigon Ward, Ho Chi Minh City

**Role:** Attendee

## Event Objectives

The event focused on several key objectives:

* **Workshop Organization:** Sharing experiences and introducing how to organize effective AWS workshops
* **AWS Technologies:** Providing guidance on AWS technologies and best practices
* **AI in Development:** Demonstrating how AI is supporting nearly the entire programming and application development lifecycle

## Speakers

The event featured presentations from experienced AWS community members:

1. **Anh Long** (Admin)
   * Topic: "How to Get Addicted to Learning Like Social Media"
   * Shared strategies for building sustainable learning habits

2. **Anh Thịnh** (Admin)
   * Topic: "Automated Prompt Engineering: Enhancing LLM Output Quality"
   * Explored techniques for improving AI-generated content

3. **Anh Khang** (Admin)
   * Topic: "Why You Haven't Started Working Yet"
   * Discussed career development and job readiness

4. **Female Speaker** (Software Developer)
   * Topic: "BMAD" (Build, Measure, Analyze, Deploy)
   * Covered modern development methodologies
